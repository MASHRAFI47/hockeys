Clients Question

Browse our FAQ section to find solutions and gain a better understanding of what we offer.

How do I determine the right size for my hockey equipment?

Consult our sizing charts provided for each product. Consider specific measurements, such as height, weight, and age, for accurate sizing.

What materials are used in our hockey sticks?

Our hockey sticks are crafted from high-quality materials, including carbon fiber and fiberglass blends, to ensure a perfect balance of strength, flexibility, and performance.

Are our hockey helmets certified for safety standards?

Yes, all our hockey helmets meet or exceed industry safety standards. Look for relevant safety certifications listed in the product descriptions.

How do I care for and clean my hockey gloves?

Follow care instructions provided with the product. Typically, gloves can be wiped down with a damp cloth, and certain models may be machine washable.

Can I customize the design of my hockey jersey?

Some products offer customization options. Check the product details or contact our customer service for information on personalization.

What is the warranty on our hockey equipment?

Warranties vary by product. Refer to the specific product's warranty information provided on our website or contact customer support for details.
